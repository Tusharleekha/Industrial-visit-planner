TATA Group.

Description:

Tata Group is an Indian multinational conglomerate holding company
headquartered in Mumbai, Maharashtra, India. Founded in 1868 by
Jamsetji Tata, the company gained international recognition after
purchasing several global companies. One of India's largest
conglomerates, Tata Group is owned by Tata Sons.
Each Tata company operates independently under the guidance and
supervision of its own board of directors and shareholders.
Significant Tata companies and subsidiaries include Tata Steel,
Tata Motors, Jaguar Land Rover, Tata Consultancy Services,
Tata Advanced Systems Limited , Tata Power, Tata Chemicals,
Tata Global Beverages, Tata Coffee, Tata Teleservices, Titan,
Voltas, TATA cliq, Tata Communications, and The Indian Hotels
Company Limited (Taj Hotels).
