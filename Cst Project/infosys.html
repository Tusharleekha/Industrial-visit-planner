INFOSYS

Description:

Infosys Limited (formerly Infosys Technologies Limited)
is an Indian multinational corporation that provides business
consulting, information technology and outsourcing services.
It has its headquarters in Bengaluru, Karnataka, India.

Infosys is the second-largest Indian IT company by 2017
revenue and 596th largest public company in the world based on
revenue.On September 28, 2018, its market capitalisation was
$44.32 billion.
